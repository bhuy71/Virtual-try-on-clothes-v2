# Example Images

Place your example images here:

- `person.jpg` - Image of a person (full body or upper body)
- `cloth.jpg` - Image of a clothing item on white background

## Expected Image Format

### Person Image
- Should show the full or upper body of a person
- Clear lighting and minimal occlusions
- Resolution: 256x192 (CP-VTON) or 1024x768 (HR-VITON)

### Cloth Image
- Should show the clothing item on a white/plain background
- Front view, laid flat or on a mannequin
- Same resolution as person image

## Sample Datasets

You can download sample images from:
- [VITON Dataset](https://github.com/xthan/VITON)
- [VITON-HD Dataset](https://github.com/shadow2496/VITON-HD)
